Our History

It was Founded by Mr. Baskaran, Star Logic Enterprises (SLE) is a prominent freight forwarding company based in India, specializing in sea, air, and trucking freight solutions. With years of experience in the logistics industry, Mr. Baskaran has built a company known for its commitment to customer satisfaction and operational excellence.

At Star Logic, we offer an extensive range of services, including Full Container Load (FCL), Less than Container Load (LCL), warehousing, customs clearance, cargo consolidation, and insurance application, ensuring our clients have seamless, one-stop logistics experience.

  • Full Container Load (FCL)
  • Less than Container Load (LCL)
  • Warehousing
  • Customs Clearance
  • Cargo Consolidation
  • Insurance Application

SLE is dedicated to broadening its logistics and freight forwarding services, aiming to deliver enhanced value to customers by building long-term partnerships with reputable industry leaders. These strategic affiliations allow us to strengthen our network and uphold the high standards that define SLE. Through these collaborations, we ensure top-tier shipping and forwarding solutions at highly competitive rates, providing seamless service, reliability, and exceptional value to meet our clients' evolving needs.
